About Us

Matri Chhaya Nursing Home was established on 13th August 2013 by B.C. Mahato with the vision of providing essential medical care in rural areas, where access to healthcare facilities was limited. The core idea was to offer women in these areas a safe space to consult with healthcare professionals, especially gynecologists. At the time, there were significantly fewer female gynecologists than male gynecologists, which made many women in rural communities hesitant to seek medical help. Matri Chhaya aims to bridge this gap by offering compassionate and professional medical services tailored to the needs of women and children in rural settings.
